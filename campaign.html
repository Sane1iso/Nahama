The Problem

Changing the common perception that Valentine's Day is only about romantic love and instead promoting it as a day to celebrate love for family, friends, communities, and beyond.

The Opportunity

The Cadbury campaign will celebrate the power of love beyond romance, featuring love letters to loved ones and a community project with Ithemba Lethu Children's Home. Set for Valentineâ€™s Day, it aims to inspire a deeper understanding of love and spread positivity.

Key Insight

Many people believe that love goes beyond romantic relationships as it includes love for family, community, the environment, oneself, and more, which they practice and desire to have.

Target Audience

The target audience, aged 18 to 35, values love, kindness, and community, enjoying Cadbury chocolate while supporting underprivileged groups. They understand love goes beyond romance, encompassing self-love, family, and friends.

Key Message

Love goes beyond romantic relationships.
