The Problem

The youth are becoming disconnected from their roots, leading to ignorance about their heritage and lack of understanding of who they are. This cultural disconnect results in missed oppurtunites for personal identity and pride in their roots

The Opportunity

Educating children about South African cultures through schools fosters pride and appreciation. It encourages them to become young brand ambassadors for South African Tourism, reaching a new, younger audience. By exploring local destinations, children can develop a deeper connection to their heritage and promote tourism.

Key Insight

The Youth are unhappy about their lack of knowledge of their culture and traditions, desiring to learn more.

Target Audience

This campaign targets children aged 7-13 from underprivileged primary schools, helping them understand and embrace South Africa's diverse cultural heritage.

Key Message

Your lack of knowledge about your culture and traditions should not stop you from learning and celebrating them.

Concept: Abahloli Bamasiko Abancane - Little Cultural Explorers
