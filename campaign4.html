The Problem

Having a reliable pen during the matric exam season is crucial. Many students face the challenge of pens running out of ink or malfunctioning, disrupting their focus and exam performance

The Opportunity

With matric final exams fast approaching, matriculants need all the luck and motivation they can get. Now is the perfect time to encourage them to purchase a reliable pen, as having the right pen is essential for exam success. BIC pens are durable and long lasting, ensuring they can confidently write exams without worrying.

Key Insight

Many students feel pressured when studying for and taking exams. Without a reliable pen, this added stress can make it more challenging for them to focus on their success.

Target Audience

Matric students from schools across South Africa are preparing for their final exams. These dedicated learners seek reliable pens to support their success and pave the way for a brighter future.

Key Message

Reliable for your exams, essential for your future.
